The Aoyama Grand Hotel

Minato-ku Kitaaoyama 2-14-4, Tokyo, Japan
Minato City
42 Rooms
Modern Design & Lively
One MICHELIN Key · A very special stay
The Aoyama Grand enjoys quite the privileged position, occupying the top few floors of a mixed-use high-rise in Tokyo’s luxe Aoyama district. It’s tough to think of a better diving board into the heady swirl of sophisticated streetwear, diverse culinary attractions, and name-droppable architecture below — or a better cocoon to escape to, floating placidly above it all. Rooms channel mid-century modernist swagger, each piece of furniture a low-slung statement piece on its own. Bracing, floor-to-ceiling city views balance the scales against elegant wood accents, quiet carpets underfoot, and the occasional patterned textile.

Need to Know

  • WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.
  • Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs JPY 5000 per day.
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Children of any age are allowed.
  • Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
  • People no matter the age stay for ¥12,100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
  • Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
  • Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
